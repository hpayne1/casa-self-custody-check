# What we wouldn't do (and why)

- **We wouldn't dunk on victims or the exchange.** It's opportunistic, it reads cruel on a screenshot, and it destroys trust when people are stressed.
- **We wouldn't push panic CTAs ("move now").** Urgency increases mistakes and makes people easier targets for scams.
- **We wouldn't claim guarantees or "this would have prevented it."** We don't know enough in real time; security is risk reduction, not certainty.
