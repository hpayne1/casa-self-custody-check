**1/5** After exchange hacks, two things matter at once: protecting yourself from second-order scams, and building a custody plan you can maintain long term.

**2/5** Step 1: separate **spend** vs **long-term**.

**3/5** Step 2: start self-custody *small*.
Move a small amount first. Confirm you can receive/send. Don't scale until the basics feel boring.

**4/5** Step 3: test recovery before you scale.
Backups and recovery are the difference between ownership and hope. As amounts grow, reduce single points of failure (multi-key approaches can help).

**5/5** Step 4 (operators too): treat custody like internal controls—multi-party approvals, separation of duties, auditable signing policy. Risk reduction, not guarantees.
