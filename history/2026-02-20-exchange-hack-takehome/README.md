## What was asked

Produce a crisis-response content campaign for CASA in response to a generic exchange hack trending on X. Deliverables scoped to demonstrate the contract-first content workflow.

## Scenario summary

A major exchange was hacked. Details are emerging. People are stressed, scammers are active, and the broader audience is reconsidering custody arrangements. CASA responds with calm, credible, helpful content — no dunking, no panic CTAs.

## Audience segments

- **victim_or_possibly_impacted**: panic, anger, shame, urgency
- **spectator_not_impacted**: uneasy curiosity, reconsidering custody
- **long_term_holder**: validation of posture, desire for framework
